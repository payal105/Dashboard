About Dreadnought

We believe humanity's survival depends on us being a spacefaring civilisation and future deep tech founders will lead the way.

Our team is comprised of digitally-native entrepreneurs, futurists and operators with strengths in AI, Space Tech, Genomics, AR/VR and Longevity. We decided to launch Dreadnought to super charge the future by helping unlock the potential of future deep tech entrepreneurs.

Our Services

Launching Startup

During our course, you will be gaining knowledge of the practical aspects of launching Startup which no body talks about.

Digital assests

You will be building digital assets for your startup such as Email, Website, LinkedIn & Twitter Profile, AWS & GCP Accounts, Y Combinator Startup School Profile etc.

Real Life Milestones

  • Y Combinator Startup School Certification
  • Greater than 100 followers on LinkedIn Company Page
  • Greater than 50 followers on Twitter Company Page
  • AWS Activate Credits
  • GCP Startup Credits

Building Product

Once you complete the course fulfilling all the necessary milestones then you will lease all the digital assets to us for a 5 month time period during which our engineering team will build a Minimum Viable Product for the idea you chose and host it on AWS or GCP and market it using the digital assets.
